Chicago


Tourist Attractions

  • Willis Tower

    • The Willis Tower, built as and still commonly referred to as the Sears Tower, is a 110-story, 1450-foot skyscraper

  • Cloud Gate

    • Cloud Gate is a public sculpture made by artist Anish Kapoor,that is in millenium park

  • Navy Pier

    • Navy Pier is a 3300-foot-long pier on the Chicago shoreline of Lake Michigan

  • Millennium Park

    • Millennium Park is a public park located in the Loop community area

  • Art Institute

    • The Art Institute of Chicago, founded in 1879 and located in Chicago's Grant Park, is one of the oldest and largest art museums in the United States.
